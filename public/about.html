Our Story

Karachi's Trusted Real Estate Partner

Al-Hammad Associate was founded with a singular vision: to make premium real estate accessible, transparent, and stress-free for every client in Karachi. Situated at C 177, Block I North Nazimabad, our firm has grown from a neighbourhood agency into one of Karachi's most respected real estate consultancies.

We specialize in residential and commercial property sales, rentals, and purchases across Karachi's most prestigious localities — from DHA and Clifton to Gulshan-e-Iqbal and North Nazimabad. Our team of dedicated consultants brings deep market knowledge, sharp negotiation skills, and a client-first philosophy to every engagement.
